# Management & Board Reporting

## Overview
This repository contains structured reporting tools designed to
translate financial performance into clear, decision-ready insight for
management and governance stakeholders.

The emphasis is on clarity, consistency, and accountability.

---

## What This Repository Demonstrates
• Executive-level financial communication  
• KPI presentation  
• Variance explanation frameworks  
• Performance storytelling  
• Governance and transparency  

These materials show how finance supports leadership decisions.

---

## Typical Use Cases
• Monthly management accounts  
• Executive performance reviews  
• Board and shareholder reporting  
• Budget vs actual analysis  
• Operational performance tracking  

---

## Core Components
### Management Accounts
Standardised pack structures with income statement, balance sheet, and
cash flow summaries.

### KPI Dashboards
Financial and operational metrics aligned to strategic priorities.

### Variance Commentary
Structured explanations linking outcomes to business drivers and
actions.

### Board Packs
High-level, decision-focused reporting with emphasis on risks and
forward outlook.

---

## Relationship to the Finance Operating Model
Operational controls produce reliable data.
Management reporting converts that data into understanding.
