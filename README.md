# Financial Forecasting & Scenario Models

## Overview
This repository contains practical forecasting and planning models used
by Finance Managers and Financial Controllers to support decision-making,
cash flow management, budgeting, and performance visibility.

The models are designed to translate historical financial data into
forward-looking insights, enabling proactive management of liquidity,
profitability, and operational capacity.

---

## Objectives
• Support short- and medium-term financial planning  
• Improve predictability of revenue and cash flow  
• Enable scenario and sensitivity analysis  
• Provide structured, repeatable forecasting frameworks  
• Strengthen management reporting and board readiness  

---

## Model Categories
The repository typically includes models across the following areas:

### Revenue Forecasting
Sales trends, pipeline conversion, seasonality, and growth assumptions.

### Cash Flow Forecasting
13-week cash flow tools, liquidity monitoring, and funding visibility.

### Expense & Cost Planning
Fixed vs variable cost behaviour and margin impact.

### Scenario & Sensitivity Modelling
Best case, expected case, and downside planning.

### Working Capital Forecasts
Debtors, creditors, inventory, and cash conversion timing.

---

## Typical Outputs
• Forward-looking P&L views  
• Cash runway projections  
• Break-even analysis  
• Funding requirement visibility  
• Scenario comparison dashboards  

---

## How These Models Are Used
These tools are used during:
• Budget cycles  
• Reforecast exercises  
• Investment or expansion planning  
• Cost optimisation initiatives  
• Board and management reviews
